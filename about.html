About this publication

The Margin is written for students who want to understand the economy they're inheriting.

We cover Singapore's economic policies, cost of living pressures, and global financial trends — always through the lens of how they affect students. Our goal is to build economic literacy without dumbing it down.

Every article is grounded in data, written with precision, and designed to be useful to someone navigating university, internship applications, housing decisions, or the first years of their career.

Editorial standards

We do not publish sponsored content. Every claim is sourced. When we use statistics, we link to the primary source. Opinion pieces are clearly labelled. We correct errors promptly and transparently.

Write for The Margin
We publish student contributors who can bring original data, first-person reporting, or sharp policy analysis. Pitches should be 2–3 sentences: the argument, the evidence, and why it matters to students now.
